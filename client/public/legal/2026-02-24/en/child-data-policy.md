# Child Profile Data Handling Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-24

The Service does not directly collect child profile-identifying details.

## User requirements
1. Do not submit child profile details (for example full address, contact details, school/class information, date of birth, health or medical data, or other identifying details).
2. Do not submit or share personal data of others without authorization.
3. Use only the minimum information necessary for story generation.

## Company handling principles
- We may block generation or remove content when prohibited child profile details are detected.
- Except where legally required, user data is handled only within the disclosed scope.
- Security safeguards are continuously maintained to protect service operations.
